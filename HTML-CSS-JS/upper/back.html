Easy:

Ball Back Extension: Place your stomach on a yoga ball while keep feet spread apart on the ground and extend your lower back as high as possible. Hold for five seconds before resting and repeating (12 reps)

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere

Squats:While standing with feet shoulder width apart, squat while making sure your knees don't go past your toes (2 sets of 10 reps)

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere

Medium:

Lat Pull Downs:Using the Lat Pulldown Bar pull down towards your chest and then back up and repeat. Adjust the weight as desired. (3 sets of 10 reps)

Location: Lat Pull Down Bar located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3

Hard:

Deadlift:Place feet shoulder width apart and while keeping a neutral spine, bend to lift a barbell off the ground to hip level and then place it back down and repeat. Add weights on to your barbell as desired/comfortability (2 sets of 8 reps)

Location: Barbells located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3
