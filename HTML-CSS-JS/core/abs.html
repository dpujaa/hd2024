Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights and mats located. Grab a mat and secure a space anywhere

Easy:
Russian Twist: Using 5 to 10 lbs weights, twist from left to right in 10 reps for 2 sets (lift heels from ground for more difficulty)

Plank: Stay on hands and heels for 1 min or more for difficulty

Medium:
Up and Down Plank: Stay on hands and heels, and alternate going down to elbows and up to hands for 1 min or more for difficulty

Mountain Climbers: Stay on hands and heels, and run in that position with high knees for 1 min or more for difficulty

Hard:
Leg Raises: Lay flat and raise legs together up and down for 20 reps, slow and controlled

Bicycle Crunch: Lay and have hands behind head, bringing your elbow to your knee, alternating sides for 30 reps on each side
