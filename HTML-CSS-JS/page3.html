Do people in different age groups tend to have different BMIs?

Null Hypothesis: The mean BMI is the same across all age groups
Alternate Hypothesis: The mean BMI for at least one age group is different.

Based on the p-value of 0.526 we fail to reject the null hypothesis and can not conlude that there is a difference in mean BMI accross age gorups."
