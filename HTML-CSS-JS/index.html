What is Gymxiety?

Gymxiety is a platform made to reduce anxiety for gym-goers who are unsure of what exercises. Check out our gym tab to see excersize options based on what you want to work ion today! Whether it's cardio or weights, we've got you covered. And for our Aggies out here, look out to see where you can find each machine in the ARC!

Why is this important?

Being physically active can improve your brain health, help manage weight, reduce the risk of disease, strengthen bones and muscles, and improve your ability to do everyday activities.

We have all personally experienced confusion while at the gym. Many times one may have the intent to maintain physical activity but get overwhelmed by the crowdedness and variety of options at the gym.

In fact, in a recent study of about 2,000 Americans, 1 out of every 2 people felt intimidated about going to the gym.

Having a single resource that compiles exercises based on workout categories can help reduce this anxiety. When confused about what excersizes to do just click on the Gym tab and take your pick!
