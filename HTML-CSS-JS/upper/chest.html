Easy:

Machine Fly:Sitting on the machine, adjust weight to the desired amount and push both sides inwards for 2 sets of 10 reps

Location: Machine fly located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3

Dumbell PressesUsing dumbbells of 10-20 lbs, lay on a bench and bring dumbbells down to chest and push up for 2 sets of 10 reps

Location: Dumbells located on six different weight racks throughout the gym, grab weights and choose a bench located in various locations on first floor. Ensure your return dumbbells from same rack acquired

Medium:

Incline Dumbell Presses:Using dumbbells of weight 15-30 lbs, sit on a bench on an incline, and bring dumbbells down to chest and push back up for 2 sets of 10 reps

Location: Dumbells located on six different weight racks throughout the gym, grab weights and choose a bench located in various locations on first floor. Ensure your return dumbbells from same rack acquired

Hard:

Bench Press:Lay down on a bench with a bar of weight 40-50 lbs in hand, and bring down and up for 3 sets of 10 reps

Location:Dumbells located on six different weight racks throughout the gym, grab weights and choose a bench located in various locations on first floor. Ensure your return dumbbells from same rack acquired.
