Easy:

Bicep Curls:Using a dumbbell of weight 5-10 lbs, have wrist facing outwards and lift dumbbell towards your shoulder for 10 reps

Location:There are six different weight racks located throughout the Arc (locations on posters), take place at any one to complete reps but ensure the weights are returned at the same rack.

Hammer Curls:Using a dumbbell of weight 5-10 lbs, have wrist facing inwards and lift dumbbell towards your opposite shoulder for 10 reps

There are six different weight racks located throughout the Arc (locations on posters), take place at any one to complete reps but ensure the weights are returned at the same rack.

Medium:

Barbell Curls:Using a barbell of weight 20-30 lbs, have wrists facing outwards and lift up towards your shoulders for 3 sets of 10 reps

Location: Barbells located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3

Hard:

Pull-Ups:Hang from a bar with wrists facing outwards and pull yourself up, getting your chin above the bar for as many reps until failure

Location: Pull-Up Bar located in the area just before the rock wall next to monkey bars known as North Core
