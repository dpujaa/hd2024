Easy:
Squats: Place feet shoulder width apart and squat down, making sure not to have knees extend over ankles for 2 sets of 10 reps, use weight if desired

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere.

Lunges: step forward and bend knees, making sure not to have your knee extend over your ankle, repeat for 10 reps on each leg, hold weights if desired

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere.

Medium:
Donkey Kicks: Position yourself on hands and knees shoulder width apart and while keeping your leg in 90 degrees, lift it up to glute level and back down. (2 sets of 12 reps on each leg)

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere.

Hard:
Barbell Hip Thrust: Choose a barbell with weight of your desire/comfortability and have a bench/block to rest against. Keeping the barbell on your thighs, thrust up and down in 2 sets of 10 reps.

Location: Barbells located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3
