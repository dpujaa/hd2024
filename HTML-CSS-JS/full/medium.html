Cardio:

Stairmaster: Go for a level 5 or 6 for 5-10 min

Treadmill: Walk at incline 6, speed 3 for 20-30 minutes

Location: Both are on the second floor of the Arc at the front side along the window and fence

Core:

Plank: Stay on hands and heels for 1 min or more for difficulty

Up and Down Plank: Stay on hands and heels, and alternate going down to elbows and up to hands for 1 min or more for difficulty

Mountain Climbers: Stay on hands and heels, and run in that position with high knees for 1 min or more for difficulty

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere

Upper Body:

Barbell Curls: Using a barbell of weight 20-30 lbs, have wrists facing outwards and lift up towards your shoulders for 3 sets of 10 reps

Location: Barbells located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB

Lat Pull Downs: Using the Lat Pulldown Bar pull down towards your chest and then back up and repeat. Adjust the weight as desired. (3 sets of 10 reps)

Location: Lat Pull Down Bar located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3

Lower Body:

Goblet Squats: using a dumbbell of 10-20 lbs, hold in center while placing feet shoulder width apart and squatting down and up, for 3 sets of 10 reps

Location: There are six different weight racks located throughout the Arc (locations on posters), take place at any one to complete reps but ensure the weights are returned at the same rack.

Hamstring Curls: get on the machine and lay down, with your feet below the bar, and push the bar up as far as possible for 2 sets of 10 reps at the desired weight

Location: Hamstring Curl Machine located on the second floor in between the track and treadmill machines lined along the floor to ceiling window.

High Knees: Stand with feet shoulder width apart and jog in place while making sure knees come up as high as possible. (Jog for 2 minutes)

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere.
