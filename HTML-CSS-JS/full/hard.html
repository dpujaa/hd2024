Cardio:

Stairmaster: Go for a level 9 or 10 for 5-10 min

Treadmill: Walk at incline 10-12, speed 3.5 for 20-30 minutes

Location: Both are on the second floor of the Arc at the front side along the window and fence

Core:

Bicycle Crunch: Lay and have hands behind head, bringing your elbow to your knee, alternating sides for 30 reps on each side

Leg Raises: Lay flat and raise legs together up and down for 20 reps, slow and controlled

Mountain Climbers: Stay on hands and heels, and run in that position with high knees for 1 min or more for difficulty

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere

Upper Body:

Diamond Push-Ups: In pushup position, form a diamond with both hands in the middle while keeping same form as a regular pushup (2 sets of 7 reps)

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and secure a space anywhere

Bench Press: Lay down on a bench with a bar of weight 40-50 lbs in hand, and bring down and up for 3 sets of 10 reps

Location: Dumbells located on six different weight racks throughout the gym, grab weights and choose a bench located in various locations on first floor. Ensure your return dumbbells from same rack acquired

Tricep Kickbacks: Using a dumbbell of weight 10-20 lbs, hold with your arm at a right angle, and extend to fully straighten your arm for 3 sets of 10 reps

Location: There are six different weight racks located throughout the Arc (locations on posters), take place at any one to complete reps but ensure the weights are returned at the same rack

Lower Body:

Bulgarian Split Squats: stand in front of a bench and prop one foot facedown on the bench behind you, squatting down and up for 3 sets of 10 reps (add weights if desired)

Location: Dumbells located on six different weight racks throughout the gym, grab weights and choose a bench located in various locations on first floor. Ensure your return dumbbells from same rack acquired

Deadlift w/ bands: place a resistance band under your feet and grab the ends on either side, squatting down and standing up to fight the strength of the band for 2 sets of 10 reps

Location: Bands are located at the equipment desk on the first floor across from the rock wall and need to be asked for and returned after use

Calf Press: Using the Calf Press Machine, set the weight to your desired comfortability and do 3 sets of 8 reps.

Location: Calf Press Machine located on the second floor in between the track and treadmill machines lined along the floor to ceiling window.
