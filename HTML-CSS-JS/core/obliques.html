Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights and mats located. Grab a mat and secure a space anywhere

Easy:
Heel Taps: Lay down with knees bent, keeping back straight bend at sides to tap the back of heels (10 reps for 2 sets)

Medium:
Side Plank: Stay on right elbow and right foot facing left side for 1 min or more for difficulty. Switch to left elbow for same time

Dumbbell Side Bends: Stand with feet shoulder width apart and alternate bending down on left and right side with weight of 5-10 lbs. (2 sets of 15 reps)

Hard:
Plank Hip Dips: Stay on hands and heels, and alternate bringing each hip down to the floor for 20 reps, or more for difficulty
