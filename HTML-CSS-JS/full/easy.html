Cardio:

Stairmaster: Go for a level 2 or 3 for 5-10 min

Treadmill: Walk at incline 3, speed 2 for 20-30 minutes

Location: Both are on the second floor of the Arc at the front side along the window and fence

Core:

Russian Twist: Using 5 to 10 lbs weights, twist from left to right in 10 reps for 2 sets (lift heels from ground for more difficulty)

Plank: Stay on hands and heels for 1 min or more for difficulty

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere

Upper Body:

Barbell Curls: Using a barbell of weight 20-30 lbs, have wrists facing outwards and lift up towards your shoulders for 3 sets of 10 reps

Cable Machine: Pull cable directly downwards for 10 reps, adjust weight as desired

Location: Both Barbells and Cable Machine located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3

Lower Body:

Lunges: Step forward and bend knees, making sure not to have your knee extend over your ankle, repeat for 10 reps on each leg, hold weights if desired

Squats: Place feet shoulder width apart and squat down, making sure not to have knees extend over ankles for 2 sets of 10 reps, use weight if desired

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere
