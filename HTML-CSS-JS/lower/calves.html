Easy:
High knees: Stand with feet shoulder width apart and jog in place while making sure knees come up as high as possible. (Jog for 2 minutes)

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere.

Butt kicks: Stand with feet shoulder width apart and simulate jogging while trying to touch your hamstrings/glutes with tips of toes for 2 minutes.

Location: Can perform these on the second floor, in the back behind the racketball courts where there are weights, mats, and balls located. Grab a mat and ball and secure a space anywhere.

Medium:
Romanian Deadlift: with the barbell on the ground, squat down and grip the bar, lifting it up as you stand up straight for 2 sets of 10 reps at desired weight

Location: Barbells located on the first floor at the main lifting machines known as functional RB Court 3.

Hard:
Deadlift w/ bands: place a resistance band under your feet and grab the ends on either side, squatting down and standing up to fight the strength of the band for 2 sets of 10 reps

Location: Bands are located at the equipment desk on the first floor across from the rock wall and need to be asked for and returned after use.
